Frequently Asked Questions

How do I invite the bot?

Click the "Invite Bot" button above to add it to your server. You'll need administrator permissions on the Discord server to complete the invitation process. The bot will automatically set up the necessary permissions for optimal functionality.

Is this bot free to use?

Yes, the bot is completely free to use with no hidden costs or premium tiers. We believe AI assistance should be accessible to everyone. The bot is funded through optional donations and partnerships.

How do I use the bot once it's in my server?

Simply use the @ChatGPT command followed by your question. For example: @ChatGPT What is machine learning?. The bot will respond with a comprehensive answer directly in the channel.

Can I customize the bot's commands?

Currently, the bot uses standard slash commands that cannot be customized by individual users. However, if you have a Special Customization Request (SCR) for your organization or large community, please contact us to discuss enterprise solutions.

What topics can the bot help with?

The bot can assist with a wide range of topics including but not limited to: programming and development, academic subjects, creative writing, problem-solving, general knowledge, language translation, math calculations, and casual conversations.

Are there any usage limits?

To ensure fair usage for all users, there are reasonable rate limits in place. Most users will never hit these limits during normal usage. If you need higher limits for your community, contact us about our enterprise plans.

Is my data safe?

We take privacy seriously. While we don't store your conversations, please note that OpenAI may process your messages to improve their services. Check our privacy statement for full details on data handling.
